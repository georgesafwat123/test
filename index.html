About Moadii

Moadii technology is a Saudi company established in 2017, that provides tech-solution services to the medical sector in order to ease the scheduling and marketing procedures for medical service providers in order to achieve a higher level of efficiency, and to upgrade medical service standards by decreasing the overall effort of the managerial part of the entity allowing them to establish their importance and to maintain better health for the community
